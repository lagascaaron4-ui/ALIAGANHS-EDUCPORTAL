Junior High School Specializations

Science, Technology, Engineering (STE)

Enriched curriculum in science and mathematics, hands-on laboratory activities, and technology integration for students with aptitude in STEM fields.

Special Program in the Arts (SPA)

Focused training in music, visual arts, dance, theater, and creative writing for students with artistic talents and interests.

Special Program in Information and Communications Technology (ICT)

Specialized curriculum focusing on computer programming, network administration, digital media, and other essential ICT skills for the digital age.

Regular Curriculum

Comprehensive academic program covering core subjects and values education, designed to develop well-rounded learners prepared for Senior High School.
